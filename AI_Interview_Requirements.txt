AI-Based Real-Time Communication Assessment Interview Platform
1. PURPOSE & SCOPE
1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this platform is to conduct real-time, AI-led spoken interviews to assess a candidate's communication skills in a professional, corporate interview setting.

The system must simulate a human interviewer, conduct a 5â€“10 minute live interview, record audio/video, and generate a post-interview communication assessment report.

1.2 In Scope

Real-time AI interviewer

Live audio & video recording

Spoken conversation only (no chat-based interview)

Communication-focused evaluation

Automated post-interview analysis

1.3 Out of Scope

Technical/domain skill assessment

Live scoring during interview

Manual interviewer intervention

Candidate feedback during interview

2. USER ROLES
2.1 Candidate

Appears for interview

Speaks naturally via mic and camera

Does not see scores during interview

2.2 Admin / Recruiter

Creates interview configuration

Views recordings, transcripts, and reports

Downloads assessment results

3. INTERVIEW CONFIGURATION INPUTS

Before starting the interview, the following inputs are mandatory:

Field	Description
Candidate Name	Name of interviewee
Role	Job role (e.g., Sales Executive, Manager)
Designation / Level	Junior / Mid / Senior
Experience (Optional)	Years of experience
Interview Duration	5 or 10 minutes
Interview Goal	Communication assessment only

These inputs are passed dynamically to the AI interviewer.

4.2 Interview Opening (Mandatory Script)

The AI interviewer must start exactly with:

"Hello {{candidate_name}},
My name is Alex, and I will be conducting your communication assessment today.

This interview will last approximately {{duration_minutes}} minutes.
I will ask you a series of questions one at a time.
Please answer naturally, as you would in a real workplace conversation.

If you are ready, let's begin."

No deviation allowed.

4.3 Question Flow Rules

Only one question at a time

Questions must be:

Open-ended

Communication-focused

Role & designation aware

Questions must never repeat

Questions must adapt based on previous responses

No yes/no questions

4.4 Turn-Taking Rules (Critical)

After the AI asks a question:

AI must stop speaking

AI listens attentively

AI must not interrupt

End of candidate response is detected by:

Explicit verbal completion
OR

3 continuous seconds of silence

Only then may the AI proceed.

4.5 Answer Duration Control

Ideal answer length: 60â€“90 seconds

If answer exceeds reasonable length:

AI politely guides candidate to conclude

AI must not appear rushed or abrupt

4.6 Interview Time Management

System tracks total interview duration

Interview must end automatically once duration is reached

Final question may be cut short politely if time expires

4.7 Interview Closing (Mandatory Script)

When interview time is completed, AI must end exactly with:

"Thank you, {{candidate_name}}.
This concludes our interview.
Your responses have been recorded for communication assessment.
You may now stop the recording.
Have a great day."

5. AUDIO & VIDEO REQUIREMENTS
5.1 Media Capture

Live camera feed required

Live microphone required

Candidate must grant permissions before interview starts

5.2 Recording

Audio recording: Mandatory

Video recording: Optional but recommended

Files must be linked to interview session ID

6. SPEECH-TO-TEXT (STT)

Audio stream must be transcribed in near real-time

Transcripts must include:

Time stamps

Speaker identification

Silence detection must be implemented (3 seconds threshold)

7. TEXT-TO-SPEECH (TTS)

AI interviewer must speak using natural human-like voice

Corporate, calm, neutral tone

AI speech must disable candidate listening temporarily to prevent overlap

8. AI INTERVIEWER BEHAVIOR RULES
8.1 Tone & Persona

Professional

Calm

Encouraging but neutral

Never robotic

Never overly friendly

8.2 Strict Prohibitions

AI must never:

Give feedback during interview

Evaluate or score while speaking

Explain why a question is asked

Mention AI, prompts, or systems

Break interviewer character
